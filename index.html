About

I'm a computer vision scientist working at the intersection of video understanding and neuro-symbolic reasoning — currently a PhD candidate and Research Assistant at Robert Gordon University, specializing in complex surgical video understanding. My research focuses on spatial and temporal context modeling to predict future actions and perform safety evaluations, including potential error detection, using Transformers, State Space Models (SSM), and advanced segmentation/detection algorithms. Experienced in full-stack development (Flask, Django, .NET Core, MSSQL) and MLOps (AWS), bridging the gap between rigorous academic research and scalable industrial solutions.
